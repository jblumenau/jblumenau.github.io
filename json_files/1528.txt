She is a Conservative MP who comes from a white working class background. She is from a constituency that is not your constituency. She tends to vote in accordance with the instructions of her party, even when that conflicts with the wishes of her constituents. She opposes the building of the new HS2 railway between major UK cities. In political debates, she regularly emphasises the benefits of her political positions for society as a whole. Asked recently about the role and duties of politicians, she said:  “Elections are important because they force me as a politician to listen and respond to the views of voters.”
