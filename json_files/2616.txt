He is a Labour MP who comes from a white working class background. He is from a constituency that is not your constituency. He tends to vote in accordance with the instructions of his party, even when that conflicts with his own beliefs. He supports the construction of thousands of new onshore wind turbines. In political debates, he regularly explains how his decisions help to promote the national interest. Asked recently about the role and duties of politicians, he said: “I went into politics to implement good ideas, not popular ones that win elections.”
