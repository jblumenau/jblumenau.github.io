She is a Conservative MP who comes from a white middle class background. She is from a constituency that is not your constituency. She tends to vote in accordance with the instructions of her party, even when that conflicts with her own beliefs. She opposes the construction of thousands of new onshore wind turbines. In political debates, she regularly explains how her policies matter to society as a whole. Asked recently about the role and duties of politicians, she said: “I went into politics to implement good ideas, not popular ones that win elections.”
