She is a Conservative MP who comes from a white working class background. She is from a constituency that is not your constituency. She tends to vote in accordance with her own beliefs, even when that conflicts with the position of her party. She supports the construction of thousands of new onshore wind turbines. In political debates, she regularly explains how her decisions help to promote the national interest. Asked recently about the role and duties of politicians, she said: “I went into politics to implement good ideas, not popular ones that win elections.”
