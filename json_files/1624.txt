She is a Conservative MP who comes from a white middle class background. She is from a constituency that is not your constituency. She tends to vote in accordance with her own beliefs, even when that conflicts with the position of her party. She supports the construction of thousands of new onshore wind turbines. In political debates, she regularly explains how her decisions help to promote the national interest. Asked recently about the role and duties of politicians, she said:  “Elections are important because they force me as a politician to listen and respond to the views of voters.”
